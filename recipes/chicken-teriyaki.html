Chicken Teriyaki

Description

Quick and easy (and only one-pot) teriyaki chicken is a perfect weeknight dinner with a side of steamed rice.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup soy sauce
  • ½ cup rice vinegar
  • 3 tablespoons honey
  • 2 teaspoons sesame oil
  • 4 teaspoons ginger
  • 4 teaspoons cornstarch
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 pounds chicken breast

Steps

  1. Mix sauce ingredients in bowl until smooth
  2. Add olive oil to pan and heat
  3. Add chicken to pan and wait for it to cook
  4. Add the sauce to the chicken and toss until it's coated
